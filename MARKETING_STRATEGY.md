# RewardFlow: Facebook Ads & WhatsApp Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive marketing strategy for RewardFlow using Facebook Ads connected exclusively to WhatsApp. The funnel is designed to drive high-quality visitors to the website, maximize sign-ups, and convert users into paying premium members.

**Key Metrics:**
- Target CPL (Cost Per Lead): $0.50-$1.50
- Target Conversion Rate (Sign-up): 15-25%
- Target Premium Conversion: 10-15% of sign-ups
- Target ROAS (Return on Ad Spend): 3:1 or higher

---

## Part 1: Target Audiences

### Primary Audiences

### Landing Page Optimization Tips

1. **Mobile Optimization:**
   - Single-column layout
   - Large buttons (44px minimum)
   - Fast loading (< 3 seconds)
   - Minimal form fields (name, email only)

2. **Conversion Optimization:**
   - Remove navigation menu (focus on conversion)
   - Use contrasting CTA buttons
   - Add urgency ("Limited spots available")
   - Social proof at top and bottom
   - Trust badges (security, verified payouts)

3. **A/B Testing:**
   - Test headline variations
   - Test CTA button colors (green vs. blue)

## Part 8: Conversion Funnel Optimization

### Sign-Up Funnel

**Step 1: Landing Page → Lead Form**
- Target Conversion: 30-40%
- Optimization: Minimal fields, clear value prop, urgency

**Step 2: Lead Form → WhatsApp Opt-in**
- Target Conversion: 80-90%
- Optimization: Automatic WhatsApp message, helpful content

**Step 3: WhatsApp → App Sign-up**
- Target Conversion: 30-50%

**Step 4: App Sign-up → First Task**
- Target Conversion: 60-70%
- Optimization: Guided onboarding, easy first task, immediate reward

**Step 5: First Task → Premium Upgrade**
- Target Conversion: 10-15%

Track these KPIs weekly:

- Cost Per Premium Conversion (CPPC)

## Part 11: Compliance & Best Practices

### Facebook Ads Policy Compliance

1. **Prohibited Claims:**
   - ❌ "Guaranteed earnings"
   - ❌ "Get rich quick"
   - ❌ "Make $1,000/day"
   - ✅ Instead: "Earn $2-$50 per task" (realistic)

2. **Testimonials:**
   - Must be genuine or clearly marked as "not typical results"
   - Include disclaimer: "Results vary based on effort and availability"
   - Use diverse testimonials from different countries

3. **Landing Page:**
   - Clear terms of service
   - Privacy policy
   - Honest earning potential
   - No misleading claims

### WhatsApp Best Practices

1. **Opt-in:**
   - Always get explicit opt-in before messaging
   - Provide clear value proposition
   - Allow easy opt-out

2. **Messaging Frequency:**
   - Max 3-4 messages per week
   - Respect quiet hours (9 PM - 9 AM)
   - Personalize messages with user name

3. **Content:**
   - Helpful, not spammy
   - Include clear CTAs
   - Use emojis sparingly
   - Keep messages short (under 160 characters if possible)
